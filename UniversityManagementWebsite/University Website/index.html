Courses We Offer

We are offering three courses!

B.Tech

The B.Tech course is a full-time four year (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree.The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Technological Applications.

M.Tech

The M.Tech course is a full-time two year (eight semesters) Master’s Degree.The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Technological Applications.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is an undergraduate degree course in business applications for a duration of 3 years. The field relates to management principles.
